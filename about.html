Mission

At Food Swap, our mission is to create a more sustainable and equitable food system by facilitating the exchange of locally-sourced, home-grown, and artisanal food items among members of our community.

History

Food Swap was founded in 2015 by a group of food enthusiasts who wanted to find a way to share their surplus produce with others in their community. Since then, we have grown into a network of food exchangers across the country, united by our passion for good food and sustainable living.

Values

  • Community: We believe that sharing food is a powerful way to build connections and strengthen communities.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to promoting sustainable practices and reducing food waste.
  • Equity: We strive to create a more equitable food system by providing access to locally-sourced, home-grown, and artisanal food items for all members of our community.
  • Creativity: We encourage our members to be creative and experiment with new recipes and food items.

Achievements

Over the years, Food Swap has achieved many milestones, including:

  • Facilitating over 10,000 food swaps across the country
  • Reducing food waste by over 50,000 pounds
  • Supporting local farmers and artisanal food producers by providing a platform for them to showcase their products

How to become a member of the community?

To become a member of the Food Swap community, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Agree to abide by our code of conduct
  • Attend at least one food swap event and bring a food item to share
